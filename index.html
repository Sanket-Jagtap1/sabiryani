About us

SA Biryani Founded in late 2017 by Mr. , SA Biryani is an Indian restaurant, delivery and catering services set in the heart of Little India. As our name suggests, we’re best known for our Special dum biryani alongside an array of familiar Indian classics that are proudly spiced with imported spices to bring forth flavour and aroma-steeped meals that’s best enjoyed in the company of family and friends.
